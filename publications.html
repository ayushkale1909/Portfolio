    Classification of Japanese Papers based on deep learning of optical coherence tomography images


    Classification of Kozo-Fibered Papers Made in Japan Based on Deep Learning of Optical Coherence Tomography Images (JTu2A.39), CLEO, Applications and Technology, May 9, 2023, Optica


    In this study, we performed optical coherence tomography (OCT) classification on Japanese paper samples. We trained and validated the Alex-Net and Res-Net to classify the paper samples based on their OCT cross-section images. Our results demonstrated that both Alex-Net and Res-Net can be used to assist conservators to classify Japanese papers with high accuracy.
